PhysicalConstants

com.phasmidsoftware.number.top.PhysicalConstants
object PhysicalConstants

The PhysicalConstants object serves as a collection of fundamental physical constants, including both exact (defined by the SI system) and measured values (with uncertainties). Each constant is encapsulated as a Quantity with its respective dimension and unit.

These constants are essential for a wide range of scientific and engineering calculations in disciplines such as electromagnetism, thermodynamics, quantum mechanics, and relativity.
