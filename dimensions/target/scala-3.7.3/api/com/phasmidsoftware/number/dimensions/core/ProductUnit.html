case class ProductUnit[D <: Dimension](left: Unit[_], right: Unit[_], symbol: String) extends Unit[D]

A composite unit formed by multiplying two units. The resulting dimension is the product of the component dimensions.

Computes the composite dimension witness for the product of two units.

This method derives the dimension witness by summing the dimensional exponents of the left and right component units in their respective seven SI base dimensions (mass, length, time, electric current, thermodynamic temperature, amount of substance, and luminous intensity).
