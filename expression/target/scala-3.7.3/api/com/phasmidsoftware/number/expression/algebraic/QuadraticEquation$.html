def solveAsComplex(branch: Int, branches: Int, realCoefficient: Rational, imagCoefficient: Structure, equation: QuadraticEquation): ComplexCartesian

Solves a quadratic equation to find its complex roots in Cartesian form. The method calculates the complex roots based on the given branch of the solution, the total number of branches, the real component (base) of the equation, and the square root value of a provided rational number.

Solves a quadratic equation to find its complex roots in Cartesian form. The method calculates the complex roots based on the given branch of the solution, the total number of branches, the real component (base) of the equation, and the square root value of a provided rational number.

Value parameters

branch

the specific branch of the solution to compute (e.g., 0, 1, etc.).

branches

the total number of branches in the solution space.

equation

the quadratic equation for which to compute the complex roots (used only for error reporting).

imagCoefficient

the coefficient of the imaginary part of the complex solution (this will be a positive square root).

realCoefficient

the rational number representing the real component of the equation.

Attributes

Returns

a ComplexCartesian object representing the complex root in Cartesian form.

def squareRootEquation(r: Rational): QuadraticEquation

Creates a quadratic equation whose solutions are plus or minus the square root of the given Rational number. The quadratic equation is defined as Quadratic(0, -r), where the first term is zero and the second term is the negation of the input parameter.

Creates a quadratic equation whose solutions are plus or minus the square root of the given Rational number. The quadratic equation is defined as Quadratic(0, -r), where the first term is zero and the second term is the negation of the input parameter.
