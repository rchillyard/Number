Algebraic

com.phasmidsoftware.number.algebra.eager.Algebraic
See theAlgebraic companion object
sealed trait Algebraic extends Solution, Unitary, Scalable[Algebraic]

Trait to model the behavior of a solution to an equation. All such solutions are exact. Typically, however, such solutions cannot be represented exactly as pure numbers. The four parameters of a Algebraic are: base, offset, coefficient, and factor. Solutions are subject to various operations such as addition, scaling, and number conversions. Extends the NumberLike trait, inheriting behavior common to number-like entities.

CONSIDER why is there so much dependence on the core module here? We shouldn't need that at all!
